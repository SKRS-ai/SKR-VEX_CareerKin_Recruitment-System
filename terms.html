Terms of the Kinship

The rules governing the Secure Kinship Registry & Verified Exchange.

⚠️ ANTI-FRAUD POLICY:

Attempting to bypass the SKR-VEX verification protocol with forged documents or falsified geographic coordinates results in a Permanent Blacklist from the registry. No refunds are issued for Premium Tier fees in cases of fraud.

1. Verification Consent

By uploading your Driver's License, Passport, or Employer Data, you grant SKR-VEX the right to perform a non-invasive cross-reference against public records and our proprietary "Employer Coordinate Database."

2. Data Ownership & Portability

You remain the sole owner of your professional credentials. SKR-VEX acts as a Digital Notary. We do not own your work history; we merely verify its existence to facilitate high-trust hiring.

3. Recruiter Professionalism

Recruiters using the "Premium Pipeline" agree to use candidate data solely for the purpose of recruitment. Any attempt to scrape the registry or use "Tier 3" proof for unauthorized background checks outside the platform is a violation of the Kinship.

4. Zero-Lag Liability

While SKR-VEX strives for 100% accuracy in our verification queue, the "Final Decision" to hire remains between the Partner Company and the Candidate. We are a verification bridge, not a legal representative.

5. The "Sony" Verification Clause

Work history verification involving specific geographic anchoring (Coordinates) is based on the best available data at the time of submission. CareerKin reserves the right to re-audit any "Tier 3" profile at 6-month intervals.
