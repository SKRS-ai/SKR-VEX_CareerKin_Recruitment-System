Privacy & Data Protocol

How we protect your Identity and Professional Proof.

🛡️ The SKR-VEX Guarantee: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification data. Your Driver's License and Passport scans are used exclusively for Tiered Verification within the CareerKin exchange.

1. Data Encryption

All sensitive documents uploaded to the Identity Vault are encrypted using AES-256 bit encryption at rest. This is the same standard used by global financial institutions to protect banking data.

2. Tiered Disclosure Control

You own your data. Our "Operating System" follows a strict Need-to-Know architecture:

  • Public View: Only your name and professional category are visible.
  • Recruiter View: Verified badges are visible.
  • Unlocked View: Only when you grant specific permission through the Messaging Hub can a recruiter view your actual ID scans or employment coordinates.

3. The "Sony" Work History Standard

When you provide geographic coordinates for past employers, we use this data solely to verify the authenticity of your professional claims. This prevents "Ghost Resumes" and ensures the kinship remains elite and high-trust.

4. Data Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

At any time, you may request the full deletion of your SKR-VEX profile. Upon request, all scanned documents in your Vault are purged from our secure servers within 72 hours.
