Why CareerKin?

In today's recruitment market, 40% of resumes contain inaccuracies, and background checks take weeks to clear. SKR-VEX was built to solve the "Trust Gap." We operate as a high-security bridge between the professional's identity and the recruiter's need for instant verification.

01. Identity Anchoring

Every member anchors their profile with a State-Issued ID or Passport. This ensures that every person in our "Boutique" is exactly who they say they are.

02. Relational Kinship

We don't just "list" jobs. We categorize talent into kinships, creating a searchable registry that behaves like an Operating System for human capital.

03. Geographic Proof

We verify work history via GPS coordinates and supervisor-locked records (e.g., our Sony Verification standard). Recruiters see the "Where" and "When" immediately.

04. Tiered Disclosure

Privacy is our priority. Candidates choose what to share and when, moving from Tier 1 (ID Verified) to Tier 3 (Full Vault Access) as they build trust with partners.
