For the Kin (Seekers)

How do I achieve Tier 3 status?

Tier 3 requires a valid government ID plus proof of employment at a recognized firm (e.g., Sony, Microsoft). Once your GPS coordinates match the firm's records, our admin queue will grant the badge.

Who can see my Driver's License?

Only the SKR-VEX verification AI and authorized human moderators. Recruiters only see a "Verified" badge unless you explicitly unlock the document for them in a chat.

Is the background check free?

Tier 1 (Identity) is free. Professional verification (Tier 3) may require a one-time processing fee for deeper audits.

For Recruiters

What are "Sony-Style" coordinates?

We use geographic anchoring to prove a candidate actually worked on-site. The coordinates represent the verified physical location of the employer during the candidate's tenure.

Can I export candidate proof dossiers?

Premium recruiters can download a PDF "Trust Report" for any candidate who has unlocked their profile for them.

How do I post a Classified Ad?

Visit the Partner Portal to list your role. Premium ads are pinned to the top of the Boutique Grid for 30 days.
