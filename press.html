About SKR-VEX

SKR-VEX (Secure Kinship Registry & Verified Exchange) is a next-generation recruitment operating system built to eliminate resume fraud. By anchoring professional identities through geographic employment proof and "Sony-style" coordinate verification, SKR-VEX creates a high-trust "Boutique" environment for elite talent and corporate partners.
